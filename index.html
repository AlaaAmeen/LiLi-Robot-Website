About

LiLi Robot is an educational game, it targets the primary stage. It can be used in school under the teachers supervision or at home under the parent supervision. One of our future phases is to automate the supervision process by combining our creative robot with a mobile application that will automate the correction process.
The purpose of this educational game is to help the students to know the letters and be able to construct words and sentences by moving the robot to the cells that contain the correct letters or words. The level can be determine according to the childâ€™s level.
This game combines education, fun and movement for students because at this stage they love to move and play with games. For more information please play the video :)

LiLi Robot Educational Game consists of:

Robot Body
Mat/Map
Digital LCD
  keybad

Very easy steps to use LiLi Robot (Current Version):

  1. Switch on the Robot
  2. The teacher/parent will ask you a question
    (ex: Construct a word start with letter M)
  3. Use the onboard keypad to navigate the robot to the desired cells.
    (ex: if the answer is MAN, move the robot to M then A then N)
  4. The letters will be shown in order on the LCD
  5. Read the word
